Sukendu and Monitas home

Requirement -

Customer required their 3bhk to be furnished for moving in. They had shopped around for partners but were confused as they were getting home interiors done for the first time.


Solution

We helped them understand the possibilities for their home in context to their requirements and budget. Our Design partner engaged with them and shared a few sample designs which helped them understand. Our partners took them on a tour of their state of the art factory and explained the quality process to them.


Result

Our partners implemented the home within 45 days of signoff.
